North Park

North Park is a vibrant urban neighborhood and one of the oldest neighborhoods in San Diego. In the early 20th c., it was a lemon grove. that needed so much water hauled over that a wagon trail was constructed from Downtown San Diego - the wagon trail is now Pershing Drive.

Streetcar lines put in in the early 20th c. by the John D. Spreckles Electric Railway brought in investors, residents, and shopkeepers. These streetcars ran along University Ave. and 30th St., creating what was the heart of the North Park back in the early 1900's, as it remains today.
